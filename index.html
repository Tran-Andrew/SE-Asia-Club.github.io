The Southeast Asia Club

The Southeast Asia Club is a club that is committed to helping students discover more about Southeast Asia. The club hosts events nearly every month, and is open to everyone that is looking for a club, no matter who they are.

The Officers

Meet our lovely 2022-2023 officers! This page lists out all our officers, along with a little description of them and what they do. These officers are super friendly, and if you see one of them in the halls, don't be afraid to say hi!
